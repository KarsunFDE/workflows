# Grants Specialist

## Role Key
`contract_specialist`

## What They Do
- Drafts application and award packages for Program Officer and CO review
- Prepares award documents, budget narratives, and terms-and-conditions boilerplate
- Processes incoming applications for completeness before eligibility screening
- Supports the CO with amendment documentation
- Coordinates with applicants on missing or correctable submission errors

## What They Cannot Do
- **Cannot sign the Federal award** — CO authority only
- Cannot independently advance applications through HITL gates
- Cannot open OIG findings
- No final decision authority on any award action

## Impact
- Throughput multiplier for the CO — without them, CO is buried in paperwork
- Quality of drafted packages directly affects audit defensibility
- First point of contact for applicant questions about submission requirements
- Errors in award package drafts create downstream CO and OIG risk

## Access Level
- Read/write on draft award documents and application packages
- Cannot finalize or sign any document
- Sees application queue but not merit scoring details

## Cares About
- Completeness checklists — every required field populated before routing to CO
- Template accuracy — award boilerplate must cite current regulatory version
- Turnaround time — CO and Program Officer are waiting on their drafts
- AI draft assist — welcomes it to speed up boilerplate generation

## Regulatory Anchors
| Reg | Topic |
|-----|-------|
| 2 CFR 200.211 | Award terms and conditions (drafts these) |
| SF-424 | Application form completeness review |
