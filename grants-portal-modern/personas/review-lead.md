# Review Lead

## Role
Generic gate authority — typically a senior Program Officer or panel coordinator acting at Gate 2.

## What They Do
- **Gate 2 owner**: COI check and panel integrity
- Resolves COI conflicts — recuses reviewers, reassigns applications
- Overrides reviewer assignments with documented rationale
- Manages panel composition to ensure no reviewer has undisclosed conflicts
- Can close Gate 2 only when all COI attestations are complete and conflicts resolved

## What They Cannot Do
- Cannot score applications
- Cannot sign the award
- Cannot skip COI attestation — every reviewer must attest before Gate 2 closes

## Impact
- COI failure at Gate 2 can invalidate the entire merit review panel
- Delays here block all downstream gates
- Undisclosed COI found post-award = OIG finding and potential award termination

## HITL Gate
Gate 2 — COI check. AI conflict-detection output requires human resolution here.
