# Grants Officer

## Role
Generic gate authority — not a distinct role key; functionally a Grants Management Officer or senior Grants Specialist acting at Gate 1.

## What They Do
- **Gate 1 owner**: eligibility and risk screening (2 CFR 200.206)
- Approves applications forward, returns for correction, or rejects outright
- Reviews financial stability, management quality, audit history, and prior performance
- Documents risk-review rationale before any application advances to merit review

## What They Cannot Do
- Cannot score merit criteria (Peer Reviewer role)
- Cannot sign the Federal award (CO authority)

## Impact
- Filters out ineligible and high-risk applicants before expensive merit review
- Risk-review decision is the primary audit target for rejected applications
- Weak rationale at Gate 1 = OIG finding if rejection is challenged

## HITL Gate
Gate 1 — screening. AI eligibility check output requires human sign-off here before application advances.

| Reg | Topic |
|-----|-------|
| 2 CFR 200.206 | Risk review — financial stability, audit findings, prior performance |
