# Selecting Official (Legacy)

## Role Key
`ssa`

## What They Do
- Holds final-selection authority inherited from the pre-grants (acquisition) baseline
- Currently mapped to the merit-consensus surface in the portal
- Makes final application selection decisions when merit panel consensus is unclear
- W4–W5 work will repurpose or retire this role for grants-specific authority

## What They Cannot Do
- Cannot sign the Federal award (CO authority)
- Does not have a first-class grants equivalent yet — legacy surface only

## Impact
- Tie-breaker authority on contested merit review outcomes
- Role will evolve significantly in W4–W5 — current behavior is inherited, not designed for grants

## Access Level
- Limited to merit-consensus surface
- Cannot access financial, amendment, or OIG workflows

## Cares About
- Clear escalation path when reviewers disagree
- Documented rationale for any selection override

## Notes
Label "Selecting Official" and enum key `ssa` are acquisition-baseline artifacts. Cohort repurposes in W4–W5. Do not build new features against this role until after that work.
